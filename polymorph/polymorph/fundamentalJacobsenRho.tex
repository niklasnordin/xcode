Solves for $\rho$ in
\[
p = \rho \frac{R_{u}}{M_w} T \left[ 1 + \delta \left( \frac{\partial \bar{\alpha}}{\partial \delta}\right)_\tau \right]
\]
where $\bar \alpha$ is the residual dimensionless Helmholtz energy given by
\[
\bar{\alpha}(\delta,\tau) = \sum_{k=0}^{22} N_k \delta^{i_k} \tau^{j_k} \exp ( -\gamma\delta^{l_k} )
\]
and 
\[
\delta = \rho/\rho_c,\tau = T_c/T
\]
$i_k = A_k, j_k = A_{k+23}, l_k = A_{k+2*23},$\\
$N_k = A_{k+3*23}$ for $k=0..22$\\
$T_c = A_{92}, p_c = A_{93}, \rho_c = A_{94}, M_w = A_{95}$
